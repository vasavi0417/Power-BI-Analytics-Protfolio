## Project Overview
This HR Analytics project leverages Power BI to analyze workforce data and provide
actionable insights into employee attrition, job satisfaction, age demographics,
and education diversity. The dashboards are designed to support HR leadership in
making data-driven decisions related to retention, talent management, and workforce
planning.

## Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to understand the underlying drivers of employee
turnover and engagement. HR leaders need visibility into workforce trends at both
an enterprise and department level to proactively address attrition risks and
optimize talent strategies.

## Key Metrics & KPIs
- Employee Attrition Rate
- Job Satisfaction Score
- Headcount Trends by Department
- Age Group Distribution
- Education Level Diversity
- Department-wise Retention Metrics

## Dashboard Features & Visuals
- Interactive KPI cards for executive-level workforce summary
- Attrition analysis by department, age group, and education level
- Job satisfaction trends across roles and organizational units
- Demographic breakdowns to support diversity and inclusion analysis
- Drill-through pages for department-level and employee-level insights
- Dynamic slicers enabling filtering by department, role, age group, and education

## Business Impact
- Helped identify high-attrition departments and potential risk factors
- Enabled HR leaders to assess the relationship between job satisfaction and
  employee turnover
- Supported data-driven workforce planning and retention strategies
- Improved visibility into demographic and education trends to guide hiring
  and talent development initiatives
- Reduced manual reporting effort by consolidating multiple HR reports into
  a single interactive dashboard
